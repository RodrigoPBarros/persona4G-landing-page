O que é Persona 4 Golden?

Persona 4 Golden is set in the small town of Inaba, where mysterious events happen inside a TV world.

Students awaken Personas, manifestations of their inner selves, to fight Shadows and uncover the truth.

The game mixes daily life, friendship and supernatural mystery.
